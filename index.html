IDEA

The intent behind this was to create a common platform for sex education, as we believe in its importance as well as its positive impact. The aim is to help young children and adults to bridge the awkwardness and hesitancy as well as to navigate young minds through a more sorted path.

RESEARCH

This one’s endless—innumerable hours of web searching, case studies, plethora of ideas and pangs of bursting myths later, there we were- working through piles of articles to get the best content possible.

SURVEY

A questionnaire was circulated widely amongst students and teachers to gauge their view on various issues regarding sex ed— from importance to types to the way of its discourse, it was one hefty task.

ANALYSIS

Responses were collected and extensively analysed, this was crucial in deciding for the next steps and the way the momentum was to take shape, this helped us in structuring our ideas cohesive to prevailing majority.

WEBSITE

The website is the “one all” place, links to our resources, easy surfing, guided access and get to know about us.

COLLABORATION

Working on the lines of our vision, we would want to collaborate with a NGO, or any authentic legitimate agency which will help us further our agenda of reaching to larger sections of the country and to support us in our endeavour to create an informative quality safe platform for better sex education.
